FAQ

What people ask

Passvarsel is built to be simple. Here are answers to the most common questions.

Does it cost anything?
The app is available for a small one-time fee. No additional costs apply.
Which cities are supported?
We cover all police stations that offer online passport bookings, including Oslo, Bergen, Trondheim, Stavanger and more.
How fast are alerts sent?
Passvarsel checks continuously. Most available slots disappear quickly, so alerts are sent immediately.
What happens after I get alerted?
You receive a direct link to the police booking page to complete the booking yourself. We do not book on your behalf.
