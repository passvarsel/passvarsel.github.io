Passvarsel is an independent alert service that monitors available passport appointments and notifies you when a slot matching your preferences appears. The service is available on iOS, Android, and web.

Passvarsel is not affiliated with, approved by, or otherwise connected to the Norwegian Police Directorate, Norwegian police, or other public authorities. Booking is completed directly on official police pages.
